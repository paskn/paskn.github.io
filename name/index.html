About my name - Sergei Pashakhin

People struggle writing and pronouncing my name. Here's a guide to it.

  • In writing, follow the common spelling from the table below.
  • To learn the proper pronunciation, click the link in the IPA column. If the link is broken, you could search for an alternative tool that reads IPA out loud.

The most common mistake in pronouncing my last name is to pronounce 'k'. The letter 'k' is silent. Why is it there? I do not know, this Latin spelling of my last name was issued to me with my first passport.

  Cyrillic IPA Common spelling Alternative spelling
First name Сергей sʲirɡʲˈej Sergei Sergey, Serhii, Sergej
Last name Пашахин paʃˈɑxin Pashakhin  

I have a patronymic. If this concept is new to you, then you shouldn't use it. Stick to the simple combination of first+last name.

Personal pronouns
